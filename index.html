My vision for my production, "Granted" is a dramatic video that would ideally be viewed on YouTube,.

especially since it is to spread awareness. This video is a representation of the worst scenario that could have happened in my life.

I would like to share it in order to show that things can be prevented, especially when it comes to your own family.

Do not take them for granted.

k a l e i d o s c o p e

.

.

In this piece, I decided to explore virtual reality with a simple simulation of a real life kaleidoscope, using the Google cardboard to display it..

I used found footage in order to mimic the light being reflected into the device, manipulating the footage by recoloring, and putting it into a 360 degree format.

Along with the visual representation, I included 8D audio to also mimic the feeling that one might get when looking through a kaleidoscope.

To me, it is a very fascinating object and I wanted to make it feel completely immersive with visual and auditory features.

My project, "World of Warcraft," was inspired by the game "World of Warcraft." I wanted to create a class selection,.

but a simpler, and more descriptive version. World of Warcraft was the first game I really played online on a PC.

This was my earliest memory of what the Internet was like, other than Youtube and browser games.

World of Warcraft introduced me into my love of games. In my website, I created buttons for eight different

classes that direct to a site of an image and description of that class.
